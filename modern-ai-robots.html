What Are Modern AI Robots?

Modern AI robots are much smarter than traditional robots. They use artificial intelligence (AI) to learn from their surroundings and make decisions on their own. Some even look and act like humans!

These robots can do amazing things like recognize voices, see objects, and even play games. They can learn new tasks and improve over time, just like a human would!
