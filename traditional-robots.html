What Are Traditional Robots?

Traditional robots are machines that are programmed to perform specific tasks. They often look like machines, with metal parts, gears, and wires. They are commonly used in factories to do things like welding cars or assembling products.

These robots follow pre-set instructions and can’t learn new things on their own. They are great at doing repetitive tasks, but they aren’t very smart!
